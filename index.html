Research Interests

My research interest includes the theory of graph neural networks (GNN), graph algorithms. To be more specific, my research interest can be categorized into three aspects:

  • In the theory of GNNs, I focus on exploring the connections between spatial GNN models and some geometry / physics problems.
  • In graph algorithms, I focus on designing efficient algorithms for graph metrics which are helpful in the graph learning area.
  • In the connections between GNNs and graph algorithms, I focus on exploring following questions:
    1. Can GNNs learn graph algorithms?
    2. If yes, what algorithms can they learn?
    3. How do GNNs learn algorithms?
